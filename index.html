I am a conservation biologist with more than 15 years of experience. I have a Master Degree in Biology from the University of Costa Rica, and a Master Degree in Management of Protected Areas for the University of International Cooperation

I have worked on both public and private sector, gainig experience in management of protected areas, wildlife monitoring, conservation planning and international negotiation, particularly within the Unitated Nations conventions
